How to play Blackjack:

1. Player and Dealer will receive 2 cards each
2. You can draw more cards using the ‘Hit’ button or stay using the ‘Stand’ button.
3. Your goal is to be closer to 21 than the dealer, without going over
4. If you beat the Dealer, you get double your money! IF not, you lose the bet you place.
5. Test your luck – good luck!
